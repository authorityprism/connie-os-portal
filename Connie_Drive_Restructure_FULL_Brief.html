The Archive

Where everything from before the reorg goes. Nothing gets deleted — it gets parked.

📁 Archive
├── 📁 Old-Drive-Dump — everything from before the reorg
├── 📁 2025
├── 📁 2024
└── 📁 2023-and-earlier
THE MOVE
Don't sort the old files. Just park them. Everything from the current Drive root goes into Old-Drive-Dump. Going forward, when something becomes inactive, it goes into the year folders. The dump gets sorted later — or never. Either way, your counter is clear.
HEADS UP

Google Drive can be a little finicky with big batch moves. Move files in groups of 20-30 at a time to keep things clean. Start with top-level folders, then sweep loose files.

The Step-by-Step Plan

Three sessions — designed to never disrupt an active deal

Session 1 — Build the Skeleton (30 min)
1
Create all 7 folders in your Google Drive. Empty — don't move anything yet. Just build the structure. Like putting up shelves before you organize the closet.
Tj does this with you so you can see it take shape.
2
Create the sub-folders inside each drawer. Follow the folder trees in this doc. Still empty — we're building the home for everything first.
Takes about 15 more minutes once the top level is done.
3
Move your most-used files — current listings, active client folders, this week's marketing. Just the stuff you touch every day.
You point to what you use, Tj moves it. Quick and painless.
Session 2 — Clear the Counter (20 min)
4
Everything left at the Drive root goes into Archive → Old-Drive-Dump. Don't sort. Just sweep. Groups of 20-30 at a time.
This is the "stuff it in the storage unit and close the door" step.
5
Drop your brand assets into each drawer. Logos, voice docs, palette files from everything we've already built.
These files already exist — they just need to land in the right home.
Session 3 — Flip the Switch (15 min)
6
Turn on the Google Drive plugin in your Claude account (MCP connector — it's a toggle in settings). This is the "Jarvis, access the mainframe" moment. Claude can now search your Drive.
Tj walks you through this — takes about 2 minutes.
7
Drop Claude's Cheat Sheet into Operations → Tech + AI. This is the master doc Claude reads so it knows your voice, your priorities, and your world.
Built from the voice work and translation layer we've already done.
Going Forward — It Maintains Itself
→
New files go into the right drawer from day one. Aly follows the filing rules in Operations → Team → VA Instructions. If she's ever unsure, she can literally ask Claude "where does this go?" and Claude will tell her.
The system keeps itself clean once the habits are in place.
