⚡ THE BIG IDEA

Google Drive stays your home base — because that's where your team already works. Claude reads from Drive like Jarvis scanning the server. You never have to change how you think about files. You just need them in the right drawers so everybody — human and AI — knows where to look.

1
Build the Seven Drawers START HERE
Create the seven folders in Google Drive. Don't move anything yet — just build the empty skeleton. Like putting up shelves before you organize. 15 minutes.
2
Move the Hot Stuff First
Start with what you and Aly touch every week. Current listings, active clients, this week's marketing. Leave the old stuff alone.
3
Sweep Everything Else into Archive
Everything from before the reorg goes into Archive. It's not deleted. It's parked. Like a storage unit — there if you need it, off the counter.
4
Give Claude the Keys
Turn on the Google Drive plugin (MCP connector — it's just a toggle in your Claude settings) so Claude can see your Drive. This is the "Jarvis, access the mainframe" moment.
5
Drop In Your Brand Assets
All the brand stuff we've been building — voice docs, design files, templates — lands in the right drawer. Now Claude reads them before creating anything for you. Like briefing your assistant before the meeting.
⚠️ ONE RULE

Never reorganize Drive while you're mid-deal. You live in those files during active transactions. We do the folder setup between closings. Active listings migrate last.
