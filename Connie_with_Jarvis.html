WHAT THIS IS

Every week, your system checks your contacts and your calendar. It picks who you need to reach out to and puts them right in front of you — names, numbers, notes from their file. When you have a gap in your day, it tells you who to call.

WHAT YOUR SCREEN WILL SAY

IT KNOWS WHY TO REACH OUT — NOT JUST WHO

Remember all that info you collect on your intake form? Birthdays, kids' names, hobbies, anniversaries? Right now that sits dead in a spreadsheet. When the system reads it, your calls stop being cold and start being warm.

WHAT THIS ACTUALLY LOOKS LIKE
TJ Wright — his daughter turns 7 next Tuesday. Ask about the birthday party.
Maria Santos — wedding anniversary this weekend. She bought her house with you two years ago.
Dave Chen — loves golf. PGA event in Providence next month. Great conversation starter.

The system reads the relationship info you already capture and surfaces the right reason to reach out at the right time. That's the difference between a task list and a relationship.

IT DRAFTS THE MESSAGE FOR YOU

You don't even have to think about what to say. The system writes a text or DM draft based on the person and the reason. You read it, tweak it if you want, and hit send. Or just send it as-is.

EXAMPLE DRAFT
To: Maria Santos
"Hey Maria! Happy anniversary to you and Carlos this weekend 🎉 Can you believe it's been two years in the house already? Hope you're loving it. Let me know if you ever need anything!"
→ Tap to send via text. Or push to ManyChat for Instagram DM.

Since your Claude connects to ManyChat, the system can push these through whatever channel works best — text message, Instagram DM, or email. You pick the channel per person, or let the system pick based on how they found you.

IF YOU SKIP A DAY

No guilt. No red badges. The system moves those people to the next open slot. Nothing falls through. Nothing piles up.

HOW DOES IT KNOW WHEN I'M FREE?

It reads your Google Calendar. Gym, Autumn's gymnastics on Tuesdays at 4:15, your showings, team meetings — those are locked. Everything else is open. You never tell it your schedule. It already knows.
