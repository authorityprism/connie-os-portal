Your First 30 Days in The Lease Line Collective
Welcome. You are here because you are done chasing and ready to start attracting. This roadmap tells you exactly what to do and when. Follow it step by step. If you get stuck, post in the community — we have all been where you are.
DAY 1

Get Set Up

Do these before anything else
✓ Complete
  • Watch Connie's welcome video (pinned in the community feed)
  • Fill out your full intake form (link in the welcome post)
  • Post in the "Introduce Yourself" thread — tell us who you are, where you practice, and one goal
  • Check your Instagram account type (personal / business / creator?)
  • If you are NOT on a Creator Account — go to the classroom and start Price of Entry Module 1
DAYS 2–7

Learn the Basics

Work through the classroom at your own pace
✓ Complete
  • Price of Entry Module 1: Account Setup
  • Price of Entry Module 2: Make Your First Reel
  • Price of Entry Module 3: Make Your First Carousel
  • Price of Entry Module 4: Stories Basics
  • Then start the Jumpstart Guide
  • Skip Price of Entry (or skim it)
  • Go straight to the Jumpstart Guide
  • Complete the Profile Audit Checklist
  • Start Module 1: The Lease Line Mindset
  • Start Module 2: Show Up As Yourself
Both paths — do these too:
  • Add the weekly live coaching call to your calendar
  • Submit a question for the next live call (post it in the community)
  • Check your inbox Monday morning for your first Agent Edge email
DAYS 8–14

Start Showing Up

Post your first content and attend your first live
✓ Complete
  • Attend your first live coaching call (or watch the replay)
  • Post your first Reel (it does not have to be perfect — just post it)
  • Post your first carousel OR static post
  • Post at least 3 Stories this week
  • Share your first piece of content in the community for feedback
  • Complete the Jumpstart Guide through Module 3: Sphere Activation
  • Start your sphere list — write down 10 people you have not talked to in 30+ days
DAYS 15–21

Build the Rhythm

Consistency over perfection
✓ Complete
  • Post 3 pieces of content this week (any format)
  • Post Stories at least 5 out of 7 days
  • Reach out to 5 people on your sphere list (not pitching — reconnecting)
  • Attend the live coaching call or watch the replay
  • Complete the Jumpstart Guide through Module 4: Community & Events
  • Start thinking about your first community event (even a small one)
  • Engage with at least 3 other members in the community this week
DAYS 22–30

Own It

The system is working — trust it
✓ Complete
  • Post content at least 4 times this week
  • Review your Instagram analytics — what got the most saves and shares?
  • Reach out to 5 more people on your sphere list
  • Attend the live coaching call and bring a question
  • Complete the Jumpstart Guide Module 5: Your 30-Day Action Plan
  • Plan or schedule your first community event
  • Post a win in the community — any size counts
  • Compare your follower count today vs. when you joined
After 30 days, you should be able to say:• My profile is optimized and I am on a Creator Account
• I know what to post and I am posting consistently
• I have reconnected with at least 10 people in my sphere
• I have attended live coaching calls and feel supported
• I have a plan for my first community event
• I am no longer guessing — I have a system
Stop chasing. Start attracting. Cross the line.
